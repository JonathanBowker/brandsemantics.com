# Brand alignment rubric

This template helps teams evaluate outputs consistently.

## Scoring dimensions
- Voice and tone
- Claims and compliance
- Persona and context fit
- Visual/formatting constraints

## How to use
1. Define pass/fail policy checks (hard constraints).
2. Add graded scoring criteria (alignment).
3. Set thresholds for escalation and publish readiness.
4. Store results and compare over time to track improvement.
