# PDF guidelines collapse under the scale of AI-driven production. Brand as code treats guidelines as policy.

Brando® — the Brand Oracle — is a high-fidelity policy framework and Brand Governance Vocabulary (BGV) for AI brand governance and content production through Brand-as-Code. It enables policy-driven control across AI-assisted workflows for automated marketing at scale.

By tokenising brand identity elements and governing rules, Brando enables AI to generate machine-executable policies that AI systems use to operationalise an Intelligent Brand Operating Model (IBOM).

<Note>
Executable standards require schema-first thinking and verified outputs.
</Note>

## What changes
- Guidelines become governed data.
- Decisions become auditable rules.
- Execution becomes measurable.
